# Comparision_Operators

## What are they used for ?

* To compare data present in columns to data specified in conditions

## Types

* Equal to (=)
* Not Equal To (<>)
* Greater Than (>)
* Less Than (<)
* Greater Than or Equal To (>=)
* Less Than or Equal To (<=)
