### 更常用的方法, 得到相同的结果。

* ```mysql
  mysql root@(none):test> select e.id,e.dob,e.name,ei.age,ei.salary
                       -> from employee as e,emp_info as ei
                       -> where e.id=ei.id;
  +------+---------------------+------+-----+--------+
  | id   | dob                 | name | age | salary |
  +------+---------------------+------+-----+--------+
  | 1901 | 1986-11-20 11:26:00 | 玛丽 | 40  | 8000   |
  | 2000 | 2003-10-08 12:45:02 | 小明 | 20  | 2000   |
